# Superstore Executive Dashboard

## 1. Business Problem

Retail management teams require centralized visibility into sales performance, profitability, customer demand, and operational trends. Without executive dashboards, identifying growth opportunities and operational risks becomes difficult.

---

# 2. Project Objective

The objective of this project was to build an executive-level retail dashboard that enables management to:

* Monitor sales and profit performance
* Analyze customer demand trends
* Identify high-performing categories
* Track monthly business growth
* Support strategic retail decision-making

---

# 3. Dataset Overview

The project used the Superstore retail dataset containing:

* Orders
* Sales
* Profit
* Product Categories
* Customer Segments
* Regional Information
* Order Dates
* Shipping Data

---

# 4. Tools & Technologies

* Converted raw data into Excel tables

# 6. KPI Definitions

### Total Sales

Total revenue generated from customer orders.

### Total Profit

Net operational profit generated from sales.

### Total Orders

Total customer orders processed.

### Profit Margin

Profit generated relative to total sales.

### Average Order Value

Average revenue generated per order.

# 8. Key Insights

### Technology category generated highest revenue.

### Certain sub-categories generated strong sales but weak profitability.

### Monthly trends indicated seasonal sales fluctuations.

### Western region generated strongest overall sales contribution.

### Profitability varied significantly across product segments.

---

# 9. Root Cause Analysis

### High sales did not always result in high profitability.

Heavy discounting and operational costs impacted margins.

### Product mix imbalance affected profit contribution.

Some categories drove volume but reduced profitability.

### Regional demand differences influenced sales performance.

Customer purchasing behavior varied geographically.

---

# 10. Business Recommendations

* Optimize low-profit product segments
* Reduce excessive discounting
* Focus growth investment on profitable categories
* Strengthen inventory planning for seasonal demand
* Improve regional sales targeting
